## 📋 Overview

<a href="#">Topaz Photo AI</a> applies machine learning models trained on millions of images to photo enhancement tasks that traditional algorithmic approaches cannot perform convincingly — upscaling without pixelation, noise reduction without smearing fine detail, and sharpening without introducing artificial halos.

The <a href="#">AI upscaling engine</a> enlarges images by generating plausible detail rather than interpolating existing pixels. A photograph upscaled 4x through Topaz Photo AI contains sharp edges, fine texture, and realistic detail at the enlarged size rather than the softness and blocking that traditional upscaling algorithms produce. <a href="#">Noise reduction AI</a> distinguishes between image noise and fine detail — preserving texture, hair, feathers, and other fine structures while removing luminance and chroma noise that conventional noise reduction algorithms routinely blur along with the unwanted grain.

<p align="center">
  <img src="https://macx.ws/uploads/posts/2023-09/topaz-photo-ai_02.jpg" alt="Topaz Photo AI screenshot"/>
</p>

<a href="#">Motion and focus sharpening</a> recovers detail from images affected by camera movement, subject motion, or focus errors, reconstructing the sharpness that was present in the scene but lost to blur during capture. The AI models trained for deblurring understand the difference between intentional bokeh and correctable blur. <a href="#">Subject detection autopilot</a> analyzes uploaded images, identifies the primary subject and condition — noisy portrait, soft landscape, blurry sports photo — and applies the appropriate combination of enhancement models with suitable settings without requiring manual parameter selection.

The <a href="#">face recovery enhancement</a> specifically improves facial detail in portraits where the face is small in the frame, soft due to depth of field, or affected by high-ISO noise — recovering recognizable facial features from images that would otherwise be unusable. <a href="#">RAW file support</a> processes raw files from major camera manufacturers directly, applying enhancements to the full-quality data before demosaicing rather than to a compressed JPEG derivative.

## 🤔 FAQ

<details>
<summary>Does Topaz Photo AI work with RAW files directly?</summary>

Yes. RAW files from major camera manufacturers process directly in Topaz Photo AI, applying enhancements to the full-quality raw data. Both standalone RAW processing and integration with Lightroom RAW workflow are supported.

</details>

<details>
<summary>What magnification factors does AI upscaling support?</summary>

2x, 4x, and 6x upscaling factors are available. The appropriate factor depends on the source image resolution and the target output size. Higher magnification factors generate more new detail with progressively more dependence on AI inference rather than original image data.

</details>

<details>
<summary>How does autopilot mode work?</summary>

Autopilot analyzes the uploaded image to detect the primary subject, identify image quality issues, and select appropriate enhancement models and settings. Users can review and adjust autopilot selections before processing.

</details>

<details>
<summary>Can Topaz Photo AI process multiple images simultaneously?</summary>

Yes. Batch processing applies enhancement settings to selected images in sequence or parallel depending on system resources. Settings from one image can be copied to others for consistent processing across a batch.

</details>
